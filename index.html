Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to WaterLife. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Data We Collect

When you use WaterLife, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication data
  • Profile Information: Name, age, gender, weight, height (optional)
  • Usage Data: Hydration logs, reminders settings, app preferences
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status, purchase history

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Calculate personalized hydration recommendations
  • Send hydration reminders
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve our application

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our Supabase database with industry-standard encryption. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our application
  • Payment processors for subscription management
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
